YouTubeDimmer

This is a project designed to 'dim' the YouTube background while watching videos. To get started, simply drag [this link](javascript:(function(){if(document.getElementById('dimmerScript')==null){var js=document.createElement('script');js.setAttribute('type','text/javascript');js.setAttribute('id','dimmerScript');js.setAttribute('src','https://raw.githubusercontent.com/thislooksfun/YouTubeDimmer/master/Dimmer.js'\);document.head.appendChild(js\);}}(\)\)) to your bookmark bar. Go to any YouTube video, and click that bookmark! To turn it off, simply press escape. If it doesn't work after three tries, simply reload the page.
